# Collages

The goal was to make 72 collages of the same size&mdash;4" x 6", either orientation&mdash;so they could be hung:

- in 1 row of 72
- in 2 rows of 36
- in 3 rows of 24
- in 4 rows of 18
- in 6 rows of 12
- in 8 rows of 9
- or the reverse
