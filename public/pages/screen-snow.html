Screen Snow

In celebration of the best snowpocalypse Portland has had in at least eight years, here’s some snow for your screen. There’s no animation or music or any other corny thing—it’s made using a little script that creates random star patterns, with the parameters set just so.

Each time you refresh it you’ll get a new image unique as a snowflake, unique as you.
